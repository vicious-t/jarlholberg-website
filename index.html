About Me

I'm Jarl Rudolf Holberg, a wildlife and nature photographer based in Norway. My passion is to capture raw and authentic moments in the wild, from majestic animals in the mountains to tranquil scenes of untouched nature.

With every photo, I aim to tell a story and bring the viewer closer to nature's rhythm.
